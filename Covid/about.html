COVID-19

Originated from the Wuhan market in China,the Corona Virus aka
COVID-19 has left the world in a state that no one ever expected.
This has already left the homes of many people unhappy.

Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses which may cause illness in animals or humans.
In humans, several coronaviruses are known to cause respiratory infections ranging from
the common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS)
and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS).
The most recently discovered coronavirus causes coronavirus disease COVID-19.
